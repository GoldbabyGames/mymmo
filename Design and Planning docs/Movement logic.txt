Phase 1 (0-5 mins) - Exploration
- Each player progresses through 4-5 "safety" tiles
- Monster encounters scripted at M locations
- Loot discoveries at L locations
- 20% chance of deviating from main path per tile

Phase 2 (5-10 mins) - Convergence
- Players pushed toward middle ring
- Water levels rise in outer zones
- Higher chance of player encounters
- Better loot in middle ring L rooms

Phase 3 (10-15 mins) - Confrontation
- All players forced toward C tiles
- Outer and middle rings flood
- Combat inevitable in central chamber complex
